Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

Q: How do I mark attendance for a student?

A: To mark attendance for a student, navigate to the "Attendance" section in the system, select the appropriate date and class, and then click on the student's name to mark them present or absent.

Q: Can I generate attendance reports for specific time periods?

A: Yes, you can generate attendance reports for specific time periods by accessing the "Reports" section. Select the desired date range and class, and then choose the type of report you'd like to generate.
